02 · LOGO SYSTEM

The Infinity Symbol

Our logo is the most valuable asset of our brand. It should be applied with precision and respect for its geometry.

Clear Space
X-Height Padding Minimum padding around the logo should equal the symbol's heart-width (X).
Symbol Meaning
The Infinity Heart A single stroke that forms both an ampersand and a heart, symbolizing the "Ever & Ever" connection.
Do
  • Use on white or light backgrounds for full gradient visibility
  • Keep original gradient and approved color palette only
  • Use vector formats (SVG, AI, PDF) for print
  • Respect clear space on all sides
  • Use the full lockup (symbol + wordmark) whenever space allows
  • Use reversed (white) version on dark backgrounds
Don't
  • Do not alter or replace the approved colors
  • Do not stretch, compress, or distort proportions
  • Do not add outlines, shadows, glows, or bevels
  • Do not place on busy or patterned backgrounds
  • Do not rotate or tilt the logo
  • Do not use at sizes where particle detail is lost

12 · SOCIAL MEDIA

Sharing the Story

Our presence on social platforms should feel like a digital extension of our memory journals. High emotional value, clean compositions, and respectful storytelling.

SOCIAL ELEMENT RULE
Photography style Real families, authentic moments, golden hour warmth — no studio-staged shots
Logo on posts Bottom-right corner, white version, min 80px, semi-transparent at 80% opacity
Text overlays Instrument Serif italic for quotes; Inter 500 for CTAs; never more than 2 type styles per image
Color overlays Use brand gradient at max 25% opacity over photography — never block the image
Captions Lead with emotion or story, follow with feature/benefit, end with soft CTA or question
Hashtags #ForeverAndEver #FamilyMemories #DigitalKeepsake #FamilyLegacy #PreserveMemories
Handle @foreverandever across all platforms
