11 · SOCIAL MEDIA

Sharing the Story

Our presence on social platforms should feel like a digital extension of our memory journals. High emotional value, clean compositions, and respectful storytelling.

SOCIAL ELEMENT RULE
Photography style Real families, authentic moments, golden hour warmth — no studio-staged shots
Logo on posts Bottom-right corner, white version, min 80px, semi-transparent at 80% opacity
Text overlays Instrument Serif italic for quotes; Inter 500 for CTAs; never more than 2 type styles per image
Color overlays Use brand gradient at max 25% opacity over photography — never block the image
Captions Lead with emotion or story, follow with feature/benefit, end with soft CTA or question
Hashtags #ForeverAndEver #FamilyMemories #DigitalKeepsake #FamilyLegacy #PreserveMemories
